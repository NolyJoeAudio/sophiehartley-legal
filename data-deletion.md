# Data Deletion Instructions

## Overview
Sophie Hartley does not collect or store personal user data. We only publish content to social media platforms and view aggregated analytics provided by those platforms.

## What We Don't Store
- User names or profiles
- Personal information
- Viewing history
- Contact information
- Location data

## Platform Data
When you interact with our content on Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, or YouTube, those platforms may collect data according to their own privacy policies. We do not have access to or control over this data collection.

## Requesting Data Deletion

If you have concerns about data, please contact the respective platform directly:

**Instagram/Facebook:**
- Instagram Privacy Settings: https://help.instagram.com/privacy
- Facebook Data Settings: https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=your_facebook_information

**TikTok:**
- TikTok Privacy Settings: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy

**YouTube:**
- YouTube Data Settings: https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy

## Contact Us

If you have questions about our data practices:
- Email: info@cradleai.com
We will respond within 30 days of receiving your request.
