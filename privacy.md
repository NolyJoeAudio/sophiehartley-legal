# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 17, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Sophie Hartley ("we," "us," "our") handles information in connection with our educational parenting content on TikTok and other social media platforms.

## 2. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information from viewers. We only:
- Publish educational content to social media platforms
- View publicly available engagement metrics (views, likes, comments, shares) provided by the platforms
- May respond to public comments and direct messages on our posts

## 3. Platform Data

When you view our content on TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube, those platforms collect data according to their own privacy policies:
- [TikTok Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy)
- [Instagram Privacy Policy](https://help.instagram.com/privacy)
- [YouTube Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

We have no control over and are not responsible for the data collection practices of these platforms.

## 4. AI Content Creation

Our content is created using artificial intelligence services from third-party providers:
- Anthropic (Claude AI)
- ElevenLabs (voice synthesis)
- HeyGen (video generation)
- OpenAI (audio transcription)

These services process data according to their own privacy policies. We do not share any viewer data with these services.

## 5. Analytics

We use platform-provided analytics (aggregated and anonymized) to understand:
- Which topics are most helpful to our audience
- What times of day to publish content
- Which content formats perform best

This data is provided by TikTok/Instagram/YouTube and does not personally identify individual viewers.

## 6. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies. Social media platforms may set their own cookies when you view our content on their apps or websites.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our content is intended for adults (parents and caregivers). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

## 8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share viewer data with third parties. We do not have access to personal information beyond what social media platforms display publicly (usernames, public comments).

## 9. Data Retention

We do not store viewer data. Platform analytics data is retained according to each platform's retention policies.

## 10. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Stop viewing our content at any time
- Block or unfollow our accounts
- Request deletion of your comments (we will delete if possible)
- Contact platforms directly about your data rights

For data rights related to platform collection, contact the respective platforms directly.

## 11. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect our accounts and content creation systems.

## 12. International Users

Our content may be viewed globally. By viewing our content, you consent to information being processed according to this Privacy Policy.

## 13. Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date will reflect changes. Continued viewing of our content constitutes acceptance of any updates.

## 14. Contact

For privacy questions or concerns:
- TikTok: @sophiehparenting

## 15. GDPR Compliance (EU/UK Users)

If you are in the EU/UK, our legal basis for processing publicly available data is legitimate interest in providing educational content. You have additional rights under GDPR including access, rectification, and erasure. Contact us at the email above to exercise these rights.
